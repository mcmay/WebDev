Usage

This is a smiple vocabulary flashcard web app for learning a particular sense of a new word.
It enables you to test your memory of the word's meaning and practice by writing an example sentence of your own.
To begin, click on a link and enter the link text when prompted.
For example, enter 1ablution for the first link in the New words with tests section above.
